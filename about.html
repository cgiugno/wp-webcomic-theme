About The Comic

This is a comic about making words and text go together in new ways. The main character is a comic character, who ends up in a 2-d world and has to do comics things until they can find a way home. On the way, they are joined by other comic characters who help them do these comic things.

  • What is the audience for your comic?
  • My comic is meant to be readable for all ages! There’s no swearing, and only minor cartoon violence.
  • When does this comic update?
  • This comic updates on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
  • What art programs do you use?
  • I use Krita and Clip Studio Paint!
  • Do you take commissions?
  • I do! Click here to email me about your idea.
  • When will this comic be finished?
  • I have the whole comic planned, and it should take me about two years in total.
  • When did you make this comic?
  • I started this comic in 2023, and have been updating it Tuesdays and Thursdays since then.
  • Why did you start this comic?
  • I wanted to do exciting things with words and images, and tell the story of the main character.
  • Can I draw things with your characters?
  • I am okay if you draw things with my characters as long as you are respectful!
